About

I'm Mike — a fleet manager who spent years buried in the same headaches every trucking operation deals with: confusing IFTA paperwork, inconsistent mileage logs, missing fuel receipts, and software that promised "automation" but delivered clunky interfaces and half-finished features.

I've managed trucks, drivers, inspections, dispatch, maintenance schedules, DVIR compliance, and all the daily chaos that comes with running a fleet.

This is my big suck it to overcomplicated and overpriced software made by money hungry tech bros.

So I built something different. I created IFTA Wizard because I needed a tool that actually makes sense in the real world — fast, simple, and made for people who don't have time to learn another complicated system.

No training videos. No manuals. No nonsense. Completely free. Just clear results.
